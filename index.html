§1State your values. The arithmetic does the rest.

This page runs the complete EPOE optimization model, including 18 spending categories, 6 revenue instruments with behavioral erosion, an endogenous economy, and the full 11-year debt recursion entirely in your browser. Set the α-weights (how much you value each outcome), choose the constraints your coalition would accept, and solve. Start from one of the five published scenarios or build your own. Every empirical parameter is documented in model/data.py with source and confidence rating.

The value ledger — α weights

Pure value statements. All outcome utilities share one unit (1.0 ≡ $100B of social value), so these weights compare like with like.

Constraints & instruments — what your coalition accepts

Fiscal ceilings, political feasibility, and which revenue instruments are on the table.

Ready. A solve takes a few seconds on a laptop, longer on a phone.

Solver: multi-start augmented-Lagrangian over the same smooth program the Python engine solves with SLSQP. Validated head-to-head: headline results match the published SLSQP solutions to within ~$10B (see dashboard/validate.js).
